Our Journey So Far

Complete Turnkey Structural & PEB Infrastructure Solutions

Incorporated in the year 2022, the PEBX group draws upon an elite foundation of 15 plus years of structural steel fabrication work experience in India. We deliver elite engineering frameworks for all kinds of Industrial, Warehouse, and Composite Commercial structural units.

We launched as a specialized enterprise delivering end-to-end design, high-capacity fabrication, material supply, and rigorous execution systems. Our systems utilize premier, verified raw steel outputs manufactured by industry gold standards like JSW Steel and TATA BlueScope Steel.

Over time, we expanded our design intelligence, establishing fully optimized production setups managed by qualified engineering professionals. We map raw conceptual frameworks into durable, structural architecture engineered to maximize loading compliance, material economy, and aesthetic value.

Our Vision

To provide sustainable, energy-efficient, and cost-effective next-generation steel buildings to the Indian industry, enhancing the lives of all associated partners.

Our Mission

To achieve business excellence through high ethical standards, exceptional manufacturing quality, continuous innovation, and structural safety.

The Advantages of PEB Design Systems

Structural Frame Profiles

Tapered Column Clear Span (TCCS)

Engineered to provide a clear floor area without interior supporting columns. Perfect for warehouses, aircraft hangars, and heavy logistics facilities requiring maximum interior space flexibility. Allows clear spans extending up to 40 meters.

PEB Engineering Benefits

  • Clear Spans: Reach up to 40 meters without requiring internal intermediate pillars.
  • Dimensional Flexibility: Highly scalable custom lengths, widths, and heights.
  • Cost & Timeline Fixity: Reduced initial layout budgets and quick turnaround execution windows.
  • Material Economy: Achieve steel weight reduction exceeding 20% compared to traditional beams.

Purlins & Channels Performance

  • High strength-to-weight structural ratios supporting heavy live and dead loads.
  • Manufactured natively on highly accurate automatic cutting lines.
  • Enhanced life cycle parameters backed by premium material durability coatings.
  • Ideal for bolted modular configurations on field erection spots.
